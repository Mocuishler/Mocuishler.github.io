Partner-Led Support 介绍

Partner-Led Support 介绍

通过这篇文章,希望能够解决以下三个问题:

  • AWS Partner-Led Support 的定义
  • 识别相关风险和常见误解
  • 比较 AWS Partner-Led Support 和 AWS Resold Support 的区别

定义

APN 管理所有客户工单(case loads)

由 APN工程师 直接提交Case给AWS [1]

最终用户 (End Customer) 需要想APN 提供账号访问权限 [2]

不再要求 AWS Resold Support (the resale of support is not required)

优势:

  • 向终端客户提供一致性的体验 //我理解就是有了统一对接人–>来自APN的指定工程师

  • 通过整合Support给APN带来新的商机 //卖其他产品和服务的机会

  • 实现额外的细分市场差异化 //和其他APN区别出来

  • Provides an integrated experience to end customers

  • Generates new business by incorporating support features

  • Enables additional market segment differentiation

AWS Partner-Led Support 能够提供:

  • Understands unique customer workloads //对于这个客户的业务更了解
  • Understands unique business or industry //对于垂直领域更了解,能帮客户加速他们业务
  • Can provide support in their native language //中文支持
  • Agrees to a specific Service Level Agreement (SLA) //特殊的SLA,比如7*24 Call Center 这种

所以根据以上的优势,目标客户是:

  1. Customers who may benefit from more hands-on support with operation of resources in their AWS accounts //需要更多动手操作的客户
  2. Custom ers who prefer to avoid the technical details of a solution and instead employ fully managed service offerings from an APN Partner //不关心技术细节,直接交给 Partner去执行的客户 (很像MSP对象)
  3. Customers who may benefit from an APN Partner’s expertise in a specific technology or industry, such as healthcare, finance, or government //在特定领域需要Partner知识的客户,比如医疗教育政企
  4. Customers who need support in their own language, local currency, or with a specific SLA //满足特殊SLA

风险

  • Customers who have previously interacted directly with AWS Support and AWS Technical Account Managers (TAMs) may feel like they are receiving less support. // 以前是BS/ES 转到PL没有TAM支持感觉服务下降了
  • Some APN Partners may not have expertise in the broad range of available AWS services. //APN 技术广度不够
  • Customers who are early adopters of new AWS services may feel that their APN Partner puts them at a disadvantage if they are unable to provide proper support. //APN 对新出的服务不了解
  • Customers moving into AWS Partner-Led Support who are solely motivated by reducing costs may fail to see the full value that an APN Partner offers. //只是单纯为了降低成本才用PL,没理解到其他PL优势(比如上手操作),结果最后不满意
